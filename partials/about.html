about here we are

i made this site to help producers find cool chord progressions and teach them music theory in a jazz context

we'll cover many things from basic tools to advanced concepts. We'll start from basic notes and scales and work towards triads and chords. From there we move into 7th chords, chord relations, and building chord progressions. Next, we start looking at extensions, modes, and more chords. Finally, we explore Coltrane changes, more modes, chord alterations, and non functional harmony.
