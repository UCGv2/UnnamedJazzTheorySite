tutorials you gon' learn today

Beginner

There are 12 different notes that can be played within one octave: C, C#, D, D#, E, F, F#, G, Ab, A, Bb B, These notes correspond to the keys on the keyboard "need picture".

The first, third, and fifth notes of a scale are called a triad.

Intermediate

7th chords are triads with one more stacked note

The ii-V-I pattern is the building block of jazz

Advanced

Chords stacked past the 7th are known as extensions

Now that you know the rules, you can break the rules. Do what sounds cool
